📋 Community Rules

Last updated: March 2026 — Please read these rules carefully before participating.

1. Be Respectful

Treat every member of this community with kindness and respect. Disagreements happen — but personal attacks, insults, and harassment of any kind are not tolerated.

2. No Spam or Self-Promotion

Do not flood the forum with repetitive posts or use it primarily to promote products, services, or external websites. Relevant links are welcome when they genuinely add value to the discussion.

3. No Hate Speech or Discrimination

Content that demeans or attacks individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic is strictly prohibited.

4. No Harassment or Bullying

  • Do not target or repeatedly antagonize other users.
  • Do not post private information about others without consent.
  • Do not use direct messages to harass members.

5. Keep Posts On-Topic

Post in the appropriate forum category. Off-topic posts may be moved or removed by moderators. Read the category description before posting.

6. No Misinformation

Do not intentionally spread false information, especially on topics that could cause real-world harm. If you are unsure of a fact, say so.

7. No Inappropriate Content

  • No graphic violence, explicit sexual content, or disturbing imagery.
  • No content that depicts or promotes illegal activity.
  • No content harmful to minors.

8. Use the Report Button

If you see content that violates these rules, use the report button rather than engaging with it. Our moderation team reviews reports promptly.

9. Consequences

Violations of these rules may result in post removal, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination, depending on the severity and frequency of the offense.

10. Rules May Change

We may update these rules from time to time. Continued use of the forum after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated rules.
